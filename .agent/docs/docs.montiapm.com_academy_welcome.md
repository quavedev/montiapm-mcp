# Welcome to Meteor Academy

The Meteor Academy is a series of articles to help you understand Meteor's performance and how to improve it. These practices and techniques were learned while working on real Meteor apps and have been successfully proven by many teams and projects.

If you haven't yet, [setup an app](https://docs.montiapm.com/getting-started) on Monti APM. If you don't have an app running in production, you can also use a Meteor app running locally, though there would be less data to analyze.

[![Monti APM overview](https://docs.montiapm.com/images/overview-2.png)](https://docs.montiapm.com/images/overview-2.png)

You are now ready to read the first article in the academy: [Make Your App Faster](https://docs.montiapm.com/academy/make-your-app-faster).

_The [original Meteor Academy](https://web.archive.org/web/20170518115618/https:/old.kadira.io/academy/meteor-performance-101) was written by Kadira. We appreciate the effort they put into it, and them letting us use and build upon their work._
